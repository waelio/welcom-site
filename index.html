Fair by design

WelcomTalk starts by creating a structured request record on the website, then stays neutral in live conversations: one person speaks at a time, everyone gets an equal timed turn, and every participant has a fair chance to present their side.

The current live web beta mirrors the two-person session engine used today, while the broader product direction stays open to individuals, moderated discussions, and studio-style formats over time.

About

WelcomTalk (displayed as Safe Talk on your device) starts on the website: the user fills in request details, adds supporting documents, and generates a structured JSON case record.

After the request is created, the platform can move into a fairness-first conversation flow with equal timed turns and structured follow-up.

The iPhone experience remains the most private path for live sessions: devices communicate directly with Multipeer Connectivity and no relay is involved. The website is the clearest place to prepare the JSON request record before that conversation starts.
